# B2B Buyer Evaluation Research

Research corpus for understanding how B2B SaaS buyers evaluate, compare, and purchase MRO software — written from a founder's perspective preparing to demo and sell Athelon.

## Research Documents

| # | Document | Focus | Key Takeaway |
|---|----------|-------|--------------|
| 01 | [Security, Compliance & Trust](01-security-compliance-trust.md) | SOC 2, FAA compliance, data governance, vendor security questionnaires | Athelon has no SOC 2 of its own — biggest enterprise deal blocker. Sub-provider certs (Convex, Vercel) don't substitute. |
| 02 | [Pricing, ROI & TCO](02-pricing-roi-tco.md) | Pricing models, competitive benchmarks, ROI frameworks, budget approval | Unlimited-user flat-fee tiers are the market expectation. $5K–$15K/yr sweet spot. Corridor 3-year TCO is 2–5x Athelon's. |
| 03 | [Integration & Technical Evaluation](03-integration-technical-evaluation.md) | API maturity, MRO integrations, data migration, mobile/offline | QuickBooks/Xero integration is highest revenue impact. Mobile/offline for hangar floor is table stakes. |
| 04 | [UX, Onboarding & Adoption](04-ux-onboarding-adoption.md) | Demo experience, time-to-value, training, change management | Demo script: squawk → task card → sign-off in under 3 minutes. Paper-first culture needs non-dismissive framing. |
| 05 | [Support & Vendor Viability](05-support-vendor-viability.md) | SLAs, startup risk, contracts, social proof, customer success | "You're a startup" is the #1 objection. Counter with founding customer program, source code escrow, data portability. |
| 06 | [Buying Process & Decision Framework](06-buying-process-decision-framework.md) | Buying committee, evaluation frameworks, sales cycle, objections | 68% of buyers have a front-runner before formal eval — and that front-runner wins 80% of the time. |

## Top 10 Actions for Athelon (Synthesized Across All Research)

### Immediate (Before First Sales Conversation)
1. **Build a demo data environment** with realistic MRO scenarios — the demo IS the product trial
2. **Create an ROI calculator** showing Athelon vs. Corridor/paper TCO over 3 years
3. **Draft champion enablement kit** — executive summary, security FAQ, implementation timeline

### Near-Term (First 90 Days of Sales)
4. **Ship QuickBooks/Xero integration** — the #1 integration that closes deals
5. **Add audit logging** (append-only table) — serves both FAA compliance and enterprise procurement
6. **Get a third-party penetration test** ($5–20K) — highest-ROI pre-SOC-2 security investment
7. **Implement founding customer program** — 20–30% discount for referenceable case studies

### Medium-Term (6–12 Months)
8. **Begin SOC 2 Type II** — the single biggest enterprise deal blocker
9. **Build data migration tooling** — CSV import for aircraft, maintenance history, customers, parts
10. **Ship mobile-optimized views** for hangar floor (tablet, glove-friendly tap targets)
