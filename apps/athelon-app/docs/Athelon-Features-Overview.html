What Sets Athelon Apart

Built for Part 145 From Day One

Not adapted from generic shop software. Every workflow, field, and approval chain is modeled after real FAA Part 145 repair station operations — from RTS release to AD compliance.

Real-Time, Cloud-Native Architecture

No overnight syncs. No local installs. Every change propagates instantly across all users. Your team in the hangar and your customer across the country see the same live data.

Role-Based Access for Every Seat

Eight distinct MRO roles — from admin to read-only — each with precisely scoped access. Technicians see their tasks. Inspectors see their queues. Managers see everything.

Scheduling Intelligence

AI-powered constraint optimization considers bay availability, technician skills, part lead times, and customer priorities to recommend optimal schedules with full human-in-the-loop control.
