# Team Delta Charter - Estimating, Billing, and Customer Flow

## Team Mission

Run the cashflow path from estimate to quote conversion to invoice and payment while maintaining customer clarity.

## Personas

- Estimator (DEN)
- Billing Manager (COS)
- Customer Success Rep (portal)

## Primary Modules

- `/billing/quotes`
- `/billing/quotes/new`
- `/billing/labor-kits`
- `/billing/invoices`
- `/billing/invoices/[id]`
- `/billing/ar-dashboard`
- `/portal`

## Success Outcomes

- Labor kits accelerate quote creation.
- Converted quotes remain discoverable and billable.
- Invoice payment workflow prevents over/under posting errors.
- Customer-facing information remains coherent and timely.

## What This Team Stresses

- Revenue leakage points
- Quote-to-invoice continuity
- Billing operator error prevention
- Internal to external communication quality
