# Team Alpha Charter - Line Ops and Release

## Team Mission

Operate as front-line maintenance staff handling live work from squawk intake through work completion and release readiness.

## Personas

- Lead Technician (DEN)
- QCM Inspector (DEN)
- Service Writer (DEN)

## Primary Modules

- `/my-work`
- `/work-orders`
- `/work-orders/[id]`
- `/squawks`
- `/compliance/qcm-review`
- `/work-orders/[id]/rts`
- `/work-orders/[id]/release`

## Success Outcomes

- Squawk is captured and linked to active work order.
- Tasks progress from pending to in-progress to signed.
- QCM review blocks unsafe release and allows compliant release.
- Release action completes with full auditability.

## What This Team Stresses

- Shift handoff clarity
- RTS gate behavior
- Workflow continuity between technician and inspector
- Speed under interruption
