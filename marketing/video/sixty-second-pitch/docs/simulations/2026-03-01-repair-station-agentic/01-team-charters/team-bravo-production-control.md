# Team Bravo Charter - Production Control and Scheduling

## Team Mission

Run the scheduling board as production control, balancing bay capacity, backlog, and cross-location workload while preserving plan stability.

## Personas

- Production Controller (DEN)
- Scheduler (COS)
- Director of Maintenance (cross-location)

## Primary Modules

- `/scheduling`
- `/scheduling/bays`
- `/scheduling/capacity`
- `/scheduling/financial-planning`
- `/scheduling/seed-audit`

## Success Outcomes

- Unscheduled work is assigned to valid bays.
- Bay ordering and board preferences persist.
- Command center settings drive predictable behavior.
- Location switcher isolates workload by station.

## What This Team Stresses

- Drag-and-drop fidelity
- Planner confidence in board state
- Multi-location scoping
- Throughput planning under changing demand
