# Team Echo Charter - Station Leadership and Governance

## Team Mission

Act as leadership overseeing operational health across locations, ensuring data trust, staffing readiness, and decision quality.

## Personas

- Accountable Manager
- Director of Maintenance
- Finance Controller

## Primary Modules

- `/dashboard`
- `/reports`
- `/reports/financials`
- `/reports/financials/forecast`
- `/personnel`
- `/settings/locations`
- `/settings/users`

## Success Outcomes

- Leadership sees trustworthy KPI and exception summaries.
- Multi-location health can be compared without data bleed.
- Staffing and qualification signals support safe planning.
- Financial outlook connects to operational bottlenecks.

## What This Team Stresses

- Executive signal-to-noise
- Role/permission and location governance
- Decision latency from missing metrics
- Confidence in reported numbers
