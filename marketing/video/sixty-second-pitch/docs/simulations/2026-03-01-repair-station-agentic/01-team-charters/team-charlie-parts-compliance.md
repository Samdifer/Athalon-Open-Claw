# Team Charlie Charter - Parts, Tooling, and Compliance

## Team Mission

Handle parts intake, inspection, traceability, tooling readiness, and compliance review while supporting active work orders.

## Personas

- Parts Clerk (COS)
- Tool Crib Coordinator (DEN)
- Compliance Analyst (cross-location)

## Primary Modules

- `/parts/new`
- `/parts/requests`
- `/parts/rotables`
- `/parts/loaners`
- `/parts/tools`
- `/compliance`
- `/compliance/ad-sb`
- `/compliance/audit-trail`

## Success Outcomes

- Received parts are traceable and disposition-safe.
- Rotable and loaner actions are reversible when needed.
- Calibration and location scope are visible in tooling.
- AD/SB risk can be triaged at fleet and aircraft level.

## What This Team Stresses

- Airworthiness data integrity
- Destructive action safety rails
- Operational searchability of part/tool status
- Compliance drill-in behavior
