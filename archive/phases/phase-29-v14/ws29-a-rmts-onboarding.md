**Marcus:** "You'll have it. Now Gap 2.

**Gap 2 — Repetitive AD Interval Tracking:**

This is the one I am most direct about, because it is the one that hurt another shop before we fixed it.

The system tracks AD applicability and compliance disposition — yes or no, WO linked, date recorded. What it does not yet automatically do is compute the next due date or hours for a repetitive inspection AD. If you have an AD that says 'inspect every 100 hours,' the system will show you that the AD applies, and it will show you the date of last compliance. It will not yet alert you when you're approaching the next interval.

That feature is F-1.4-A. It is the anchor feature of v1.4 Sprint 1. Devraj Anand is building it right now, this phase. It will ship before Phase 29 closes.

Until it ships, I am going to give you a specific list — today, on this call — of the repetitive ADs that apply to your PT6A fleet. I want you to track those intervals manually the same way you already do, but using the reference list I give you. When F-1.4-A ships, you'll migrate that tracking into the system and the alert engine takes over. The manual period is bridged, not replaced."
