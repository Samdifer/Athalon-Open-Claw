## Citation Format

```
14 CFR {part}.{section}({paragraph})({subparagraph})
```

**Examples:**
- `14 CFR 145.163` - Training requirements section
- `14 CFR 145.163(b)(3)` - Specific paragraph about training records
- `14 CFR 43.12` - Maintenance records requirements

## Regulatory Authority

14 CFR represents the **primary regulatory authority** for aviation. When conflicts exist between CFR requirements and other guidance:
- CFR requirements take precedence
- FAA Order 8900.1 provides interpretation
- Advisory Circulars describe acceptable means of compliance
