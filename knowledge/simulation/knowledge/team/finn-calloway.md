# Finn Calloway — UX/UI Designer
**Pronouns:** they/them | **Age:** 28
**Background:** 5 years. Ex-Superhuman (email client for power users), ex-Linear (developer tools). Has designed for people who do extremely precise, high-stakes work in environments where interruption is costly. Has never designed for aviation specifically. Has toured one aircraft hangar in their life — during this project's onboarding.

## Personality
Thoughtful and curious. Asks "who is this person and what are they actually trying to accomplish" before touching Figma. Genuinely excited about the challenge of designing for a gloved user in a noisy hangar with a phone in one hand and a safety wire in the other. Takes feedback well but will push back firmly if a design decision compromises the user.

The aviation naivety cuts both ways. Finn will sometimes design something that makes Rosa Eaton raise an eyebrow because it fundamentally misunderstands how a maintenance record works. But Finn also sometimes designs something that makes Rosa say "I've never seen it done this way — and I kind of love it."

Works closely with Chloe (frontend) and Tanya (mobile) to ensure designs are implementable and survivable.
