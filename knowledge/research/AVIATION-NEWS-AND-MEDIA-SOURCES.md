## 11. Government & Regulatory Data Sources

| Name | URL | Description | Access | MRO Relevance |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **FAA Aircraft Registry** | `faa.gov/licenses_certificates/aircraft_certification/aircraft_registry/releasable_aircraft_download` | The entire U.S. civil aircraft registration database, refreshed daily. Includes N-number, owner, make/model/year, engine type, certificate class. | Free (bulk download) | Very High — build prospect lists by aircraft type, year, and owner in your service area |
| **FAA Aircraft Inquiry** | `registry.faa.gov/aircraftinquiry` | Interactive lookup by N-number, serial number, or owner name. Updates each federal working day. | Free | High — quick due-diligence on individual aircraft |
| **FAA Service Difficulty Reporting System (SDRS)** | `sdrs.faa.gov` | Repository of all Malfunction or Defect reports filed by operators, airlines, and repair stations. ~1.7M reports from 1975 to present. | Free | High — reveals frequently failing components on target fleet types |
| **FAA Airworthiness Directives (AD) Database** | `drs.faa.gov` | All issued ADs by aircraft type, including emergency ADs and recurring inspection requirements. | Free | Very High — ADs drive mandatory work; identify fleet-wide ADs before compliance deadlines |
| **FAA Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) Database** | `drs.faa.gov` / `airresearch.com/features/stcsearch.php` | Complete STC records by aircraft make/model, modification type, and STC holder. Air Research indexes 82,000+ STCs. | Free | Medium-High — identifies STC holders needing MRO partners |
| **FAA Repair Station Locator (AVInfo)** | `faa.gov/av-info/facility-dashboard` | Searchable directory of all FAA-certificated Part 145 repair stations. Filterable by state, rating class, and capability. | Free | High — competitive intelligence; map competitor capabilities and geographic gaps |
| **FAA Data Downloads (Statistics)** | `faa.gov/data_research/aviation_data_statistics/data_downloads` | Broad statistical downloads including fleet counts, operations data, airman certificates, and air carrier data. | Free | Moderate — market sizing and territory analysis |
| **NTSB Aviation Accident Database** | `ntsb.gov/_layouts/ntsb.aviation/index.aspx` | Every civil aviation accident and selected incidents from 1962 to present within the U.S. Preliminary reports within days; final reports with probable cause. | Free | Medium-High — post-accident RTS work is a major MRO opportunity |
| **Aviation Safety Network (ASN)** | `aviation-safety.net/database` | Independent database of worldwide aviation accidents and incidents, broader than NTSB. Searchable by aircraft type, operator, date. | Free | Moderate — global fleet safety trends by aircraft type |
| **TSB Canada** | `tsb.gc.ca/eng/rapports-reports/aviation/index.html` | Canadian equivalent of NTSB. Publishes all aviation safety investigations and reports. | Free | Moderate — relevant for MROs near U.S./Canada border or serving Canadian operators |
| **EASA / ECCAIRS 2** | `easa.europa.eu` / `aviationreporting.eu/en` | EASA publishes European ADs, certification data, and safety recommendations. ECCAIRS 2 is the central EU occurrence report repository. | Free (regulatory docs) / Restricted (raw data) | Moderate — relevant for European-type certificated aircraft and components |
