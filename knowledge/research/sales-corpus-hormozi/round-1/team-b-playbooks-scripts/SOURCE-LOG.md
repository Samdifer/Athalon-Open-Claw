## SOURCE 1
- **URL:** https://naveens-organization-10.gitbook.io/naveennotes/alex-hormozi-notes/hormozis-pre-sales-opening-and-closing-playbook
- **Type:** Community notes / blog (derivative of Hormozi podcast/YouTube content)
- **Retrieved:** 2026-03-07
- **Key Claims:**
  - Pre-sales: sale starts at first customer interaction (ad, website click)
  - Setter → Closer edification process
  - BANT qualification (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing)
  - Outbound intrigue script: "Hey [Business Name], I'm calling about [Competitor]. Give me a call back."
  - Inbound opener: "You weren't a crazy person on the internet, ha…" + immediate time-slot offer
  - Opening framework: "Proof, Promise, Plan"
  - Closing starts at first "No" — embrace resistance
  - When they say YES: "Shut up and close" — stop talking immediately
  - Rapid-fire questions: "What's your main concern?" / "What are you afraid of having happen?" / "What variables are you using to make this decision?" / "What makes this a yes?" / "What makes this a no?"
  - AAA framework referenced: Acknowledge, Associate, Ask
  - Detail-deflection: "Question the question" technique
- **Confidence:** HIGH (consistent with Hormozi's own podcast/YouTube content)

## SOURCE 4
- **URL:** https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/the-game-w-alex-hormozi-2024-12-20-episode-summary-closing-made-easy-the-triple-a-framework-explained-ep-810
- **Type:** Podcast episode summary — "The Game w/ Alex Hormozi" Ep 810, December 2024
- **Retrieved:** 2026-03-07
- **Key Claims:**
  - AAA Framework: Acknowledge (repeat objection neutrally) → Associate (connect to past buyers who had same concern and succeeded) → Ask (confidently re-ask for the sale)
  - "Reframe objections as preferences" — objection often reveals how prospect wants to achieve goal, not refusal to buy
  - "Guide prospects to conclusions" — use questions to let them arrive at YES themselves
  - Avoid unplanned answers: "like a lawyer never asking questions without knowing the answers"
- **Confidence:** VERY HIGH (direct podcast summary from primary source Ep 810)
